ABOUT US


We at ReBooks have taken the initiative to make the journey of students simpler. We have created a platform where the students will be able to buy or sell used books,notes,xerox books and other stationary material like calculators etc. We have felt the necessity of selling and buying these reused stuff because it would cost much less than buying the original stuff itself.. For a college student affording new stuff is not possible everytime. By our website, user can find their best possible match.

How does it works-


* The seller should upload the photo of the product they want to sell.
* The buyer can surf and search for the product they want to get.
* We will fix a meet up time for the for both buyer and seller.this meet us time will generally be in the college time.
* All the transaction will be dome offline by the buyer and seller when they agree for the deal and we do not take responsibility of the transaction ,so the users are adviced to be sure of the deal
